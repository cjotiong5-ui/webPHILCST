Mission & Vision

The guiding principles that shape everything we do at PhilCST — forging leaders for a complex world.

01

Our Mission

PhilCST provides quality education to students who are imbued with strong moral character through a well-balanced research and community oriented learning environment that develops critical thinking for maximum development of an individual's talents and capabilities.

02

Our Vision

PhilCST envisions to produce graduates fully equipped with knowledge, values, and skills, and who are globally competitive in their profession — ever ready to render quality services to society and the nation.

Brief History

A Tradition of Excellence

1994 Founded

On July 16, 1990, a powerful earthquake devastated Dagupan City, damaging leading educational institutions. These circumstances led Mrs. Lourdes S. Fernandez — inspired by the national vision of "Philippines 2000" — to establish a school to serve students and professionals.

In 1993, the Founder built a four-storey building in Nalsian, Calasiao, Pangasinan — the Philippine College of Science and Technology — the first college institution in the municipality, just 5 kilometers from Dagupan City.

1990
The Dagupan earthquake inspires a vision for accessible quality education.
1993
Construction of the four-storey building in Nalsian, Calasiao begins.
1995
PhilCST formally begins operations, offering degree and non-degree courses.
Today
30+ years of excellence, 12,000+ alumni, 7 colleges, and counting.
